Totally Normal Photobooth

Totally Normal Photobooth was a fall 2021 project for Alternate Arcade Interfaces. It consisted of a booth, an animatronic head with a button, a display monitor, and a webcam, all hooked up to a laptop. The user enters the booth, presses the button, and is instructed by the head to make expressions for photos. The webcam then uses facial recognition to track the user, and takes a photo when it detects the user blinked; displaying the unflattering photos on the monitor outside.
