ANCH flower genotypes

I'll just use part of a guide I made for breeding blue roses in Animal Crossing as the example. It was inspired by a guide graphic that I found errors in, linked here.

Roses have 4 pairs of genes, each with 2 variants. Genotype is represented by binary, with 0 and 1 as the options for each; but most guides shorten it to ternary(?). With that, a single value represents a gene pair, where the number = how many '1's are in the pair. ex:

  • 00-00-01-00 becomes 0-0-1-0.
  • 11-11-11-00 becomes 2-2-2-0.

Seed flowers

Seed flowers always have a set genotype, so it’s best to start from them to have some control over what you’re doing. Island flowers or ones you’ve hybridized could have way different genotypes.

Seed genotypes:

  • Seed Reds: 2-0-0-1
  • Seed Whites: 0-0-1-0
  • Seed Yellows: 0-2-0-0
